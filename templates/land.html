Frequently Asked Questions

Why should you use Billntrade.com, as a professional tradesperson?

No product currently exists like ours, for the trade.

Our product is uniquely designed to help tradespeople manage work at a lower cost.

(1) We don't charge for any expressions of interest in (RFQ) leads.

(2) We don't charge for operating in different trade categories.

(3) We remove middleman bottlenecks and costs for lead generation.

(4) Our product supports how you manage your business and support your customers.

Why should you use Billntrade.com, as a customer looking for a professional tradesperson?

We are the first to offer a new way to find tradespeople and match them to you.

(1) Once setup, the whole process is simple, just the click of a few buttons.

(2) You can select preferred tradespeople that you feel should quote for jobs you have, and reassuringly, send requests directly to them.

(3) If you don't have preferred tradespeople, no problem! post your requests to the marketplace, we can help by matching tradespeople with your request.

(4) Problem solved, now track your job and deliverables, in a simple and uniquely visual way.

(5) We understand it's your money, so! your job, your way, your say.

How much does it cost?

Our services are free for customers to use, for professional trade service businesses, after your 14 day free trial period ends, our subscription prices start from as little as 67 pence per day, per system user + VAT.

Contact us for pricing, if you are a medium or large customer, or if you are a medium or large professional trade service business.

Who can use the product?

Professional sole tradespeople, small independent contract trade businesses, homeowners and private landlords.

Use your desktop, laptop or tablet for the best customer experience.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Our priority is protecting customers data. To do this, our technology stack includes data encryption protocals, security layering and authentication protocals. We have also integrated cloud based data security measures from a leading global PCI compliant, software service provider.
